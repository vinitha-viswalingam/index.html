The tiger is a large and powerful wild animal.
It has orange fur with black stripes.
Tigers are carnivores, so they eat meat.
They usually hunt animals like deer and wild boar.
Tigers live in forests, grasslands, and mangrove swamps.
They are very good swimmers and can run fast.
The tiger is the national animal of India.
Tigers are endangered and need protection to survive.
